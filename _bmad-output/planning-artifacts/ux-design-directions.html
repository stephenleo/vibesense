VibeSense — Design Directions

6 layout approaches · VOID theme · Same features, radically different spatial decisions

Direction 01
WHISPER
Near-invisible HUD. VibeSense disappears into the background until you need it. The only persistent UI is a single slim pill at the bottom of the editor. Everything else activates on-demand via controller.
